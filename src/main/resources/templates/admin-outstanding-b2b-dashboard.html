About B2B Outstanding

B2B Receivables

Money owed to you from business customers with Kaccha (credit) billing terms. These are typically larger orders with extended payment terms.

Purchase Order Payables

Money you owe to suppliers for purchase orders. These represent your outstanding supplier obligations and need to be managed carefully.

Overdue Items

Items that have exceeded their payment terms. Critical overdue items (>30 days) require immediate attention to maintain good business relationships.

Dashboard Purpose

This dashboard focuses on business-to-business transactions, helping you track credit sales and supplier obligations separately from retail operations.
